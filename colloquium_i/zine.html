Introduction

Section 1: Rethinking Authorship

Witness the arc of human creativity as it stretches from the lone genius of yesteryears to the vibrant, collaborative endeavors of today. This shift isn't merely a trend, but a testament to the evolving nature of creativity, now intertwined and dependent on numerous minds and inputs.

The romantic vision of the solitary genius producing masterpieces in isolation has long held sway over our perception of authorship. However, this view is gradually being replaced by a recognition of the collaborative nature of creativity, leading us to a profound redefinition of authorship.
